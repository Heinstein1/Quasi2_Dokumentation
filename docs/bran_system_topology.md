# System topology

## Energy media

### Electricity
* `m_e_ac_230v`: Household electricity at 230V AC and 50/60Hz.

### Chemicals - Gasses
* `m_c_g_natgas`: A natural gas mix available through the public gas grid.
* `m_c_g_h2`: Pure hydrogen gas.
* `m_c_g_o2`: Pure oxygen gas.
